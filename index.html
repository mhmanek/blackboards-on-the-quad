Blackboards on the Quad

Grab a piece of chalk or a marker, step outside, and solve problems in the fresh air!

Why we started this: We (Gabriel Thomas and Malhar Manek) noticed that so many of us love collaborating on problems and working in fresh air—but being stuck indoors at a desk sucks! “Blackboards on the Quad” brings the fun of working outdoors—right under the sky.

How it works: Bring your own chalk or marker. Pay what you want (suggested payment $5 per hour). Erase when you leave. Feedback invited.
